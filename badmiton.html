About Vijay Dance Studio

Welcome to VDS

Vijay Dance Studio (VDS) is a premier unisex dance studio offering a wide range of dance services and events. Founded by P. Vijayakumar, MBA., LLB, our studio has become a hub for dance enthusiasts in Thiruvannamalai.

With our experienced team, including choreographer J. Vasanth Kumar, we bring your dance dreams to life, whether it's for a wedding, birthday, or any special occasion.
