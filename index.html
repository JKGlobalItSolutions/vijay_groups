Why Choose PS Vijay Mahal A/C & Vijay's World

At PS Vijay Mahal A/C, we provide a variety of comfortable A/C and Non-A/C rooms, as well as a versatile event space for weddings, receptions, and special occasions. Meanwhile, Vijay's World offers an all-in-one experience with facilities such as a modern gym, badminton courts, engaging dance classes, and a dedicated area for **VIT Computers**, where you can find top-quality computers and services. Discover the perfect blend of comfort, entertainment, and technology under one roof.

01 What facilities are available at PS Vijay Mahal A/C?

We provide both A/C and Non-A/C rooms, event spaces for weddings, receptions, and birthday parties, along with amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, 24/7 hot water, and ample parking for our guests.

02 What services does Vijay's World offer?

Vijay's World is equipped with a state-of-the-art gym, dedicated badminton courts, and a variety of dance classes, including Zumba and classical dance. Additionally, we feature **VIT Computers**, providing sales and services for a range of computers and related products.

03 How can I book rooms or inquire about events?

To book rooms at PS Vijay Mahal A/C or inquire about event bookings, please fill out the contact form on our website, or reach us directly at 8148028929 or 7010171509. For quick assistance, you can also message us on WhatsApp!
